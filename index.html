Frequently Asked Questions About Gutter Cleaning

How often should I have my gutters cleaned in Austin?
In Austin, we typically recommend having your gutters cleaned at least twice per year - once in late fall after most leaves have fallen, and again in spring to remove oak pollen and debris. However, if your property has many trees, especially live oaks, you may need more frequent cleaning to prevent clogs and water damage. Full Color Cleaners can assess your specific situation and recommend an appropriate maintenance schedule.
What's included in your gutter cleaning service?
Our gutter cleaning service includes removing all debris from gutters and downspouts, ensuring proper water flow, and a basic inspection of your gutter system. We hand-remove debris rather than using blowers when appropriate, and we always clean up any mess from the service. We'll also alert you to any obvious issues we notice with your gutters during cleaning.
Do you offer both residential and commercial services in Austin?
Yes, Full Color Cleaners serves both residential and commercial properties throughout Austin. Whether you need gutter cleaning for your home or business, we can handle projects of various sizes. Contact us for a free quote tailored to your specific needs.
When is the best time to schedule Christmas light installation?
For Christmas light installation in Austin, we recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks before you want your lights displayed. Most of our clients schedule installations between late October and early November to ensure their displays are ready for the holiday season. Early booking is especially important due to high demand during the holiday season.
Can you clean my windows and gutters on the same day?
Yes, we can combine window cleaning and gutter cleaning services on the same day for your convenience. This is actually a popular request since both services often complement each other. Contact us for a free quote on combining these services, which can often be more efficient than scheduling them separately.
